## What is PhotoVoice?

PhotoVoice is a **participatory action research methodology** that empowers community members to:

1. **Document** their experiences through photography
2. **Reflect** critically on personal and community issues
3. **Share** their perspectives to drive social change

PhotoBoard implements this methodology by providing a mobile-first platform where participants can capture photos with context, reflect using the **SHOWED framework**, and contribute to a shared community dialogue.

## Technology Stack

- **Frontend**: Flutter (Dart) - cross-platform mobile development
- **Backend**: Supabase - authentication, PostgreSQL database, and cloud storage
- **State Management**: Provider pattern for reactive state
- **Architecture**: Clean Architecture with feature-based modules

## Who is PhotoBoard For?

<Note>
PhotoBoard is designed for researchers, community organizers, and participants engaged in participatory action research projects using PhotoVoice methodology.
</Note>

- **Researchers**: Collect and analyze qualitative visual data
- **Community Members**: Share lived experiences and perspectives
- **Educators**: Facilitate critical reflection and dialogue
- **Organizations**: Document community needs and assets
